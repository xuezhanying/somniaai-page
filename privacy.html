Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026 · Last updated: June 22, 2026
Somniary AI ("we", "us", "our") operates the Somniary AI iOS application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes, how it is used, and the rights you have over it. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

At a Glance

  • We do NOT collect: names, emails, phone numbers, location, contacts, photos, health data, or any account information. The App has no login system.
  • We DO process: text you type into the AI Night Sanctuary, a random local device ID, a short-lived one-way hash of the source IP address, language preference, and StoreKit transaction identifiers and signed transaction data used to verify Premium entitlement with Apple.
  • We do NOT sell any data to anyone, and we do NOT use data for advertising.
  • AI provider: DeepSeek (Hangzhou DeepSeek Artificial Intelligence Co., Ltd., mainland China). Your story request is processed in real time and not stored by us.
  • Your rights: You can request deletion, access, or export of your data at any time — see Section 8.

3. How Information Is Used

  • To display the App interface in your chosen language.
  • To provide text-to-speech playback of generated stories (processed locally on device by iOS system speech engine).

4. AI Story Generation & Third-Party Processors

4.1 How AI story generation works

When you tap "Generate Story" in the AI Night Sanctuary:

  1. The App sends your input text, language preference, local random device ID and, when available, the current StoreKit transaction ID and signed JWS via HTTPS to our proxy hosted on Cloudflare Workers.
  2. The proxy sends the transaction ID to Apple's App Store Server API to verify the Bundle ID, Product ID, expiration and revocation status. The client cannot grant itself Premium access.
  3. The proxy server injects our API key and forwards the request to DeepSeek (operated by Hangzhou DeepSeek Artificial Intelligence Co., Ltd., with servers located in mainland China).
  4. DeepSeek generates the story and streams it back through our proxy to your device in real time.
  5. The generated story is displayed in the App and may be read aloud by the iOS system text-to-speech engine if you tap "Play".

4.2 What is sent to DeepSeek

The following data is forwarded to DeepSeek for each story request:

  • The text you typed (your story theme/mood input).
  • The target language (e.g., English, Traditional Chinese).
  • The system prompt instructing DeepSeek to write a calming bedtime story.

NOT sent to DeepSeek: your device ID, subscription status, IP address (Cloudflare masks your origin IP before forwarding), or any other personal information.

5. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Because the App is offered to users in Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, Australia, Canada, and the United States, and because our AI provider (DeepSeek) processes data in mainland China, your story input is transferred outside your home region for processing. By using the AI Night Sanctuary feature, you consent to this cross-border transfer.

  • From HK/Macau/TW → China: DeepSeek processes data in mainland China. Under PDPO (HK), PDPA (Macau), and the Taiwan Personal Data Protection Act, we rely on your consent as the legal basis for this transfer.
  • From US → China: There is no comprehensive federal restriction; you consent to the transfer.
  • From Canada → China: PIPEDA permits cross-border transfer with notice and consent, which this policy provides.
  • From Australia → China: The Australian Privacy Principles (APP 8) require reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipients handle data in line with APPs; we rely on DeepSeek's published privacy commitments and our minimal-data approach (no personal identifiers sent).

6. Data Retention

  • Your input text: Not stored on our proxy server. It is forwarded to DeepSeek and discarded after the story is generated. DeepSeek may retain the input according to its own retention policy (see DeepSeek's privacy policy).
  • Generated story: Stored only in your device's memory and dismissed when you leave the screen. Not uploaded anywhere.
  • Rate-limit identifiers: SHA-256 hashes of the random device ID and source IP address, together with counters, are stored in Cloudflare KV for up to 25 hours and then automatically deleted. We do not store the raw IP address in KV.
  • StoreKit transaction data: The signed transaction is used transiently for verification and is not written to our database. A one-way hash of a successfully verified transaction ID may be cached in Cloudflare KV for up to 10 minutes to reduce repeated Apple requests.
  • Language preference: Stored locally on your device via UserDefaults. Deleting the App erases it.

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your data. We do NOT share data with advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics trackers. The only parties who receive any data are the three providers listed in Section 4.3, and only the minimum needed to deliver the feature.
