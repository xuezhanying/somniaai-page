Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026. SomniaAI is designed for everyday relaxation and bedtime entertainment. This policy explains what information is processed and how it is protected.

1. Information we process

  • Text you enter: SomniaAI may process text you type into the AI Night Sanctuary to generate a bedtime story.
  • Voice and speech: If voice features are used, audio is intended only for real-time speech processing and should not be retained as raw audio.
  • Subscription status: Purchases are handled by Apple StoreKit. We receive only the subscription entitlement status needed to unlock Premium features.
  • Local preferences: Language selection and app settings may be stored locally on your device.

2. How information is used

Information is used to provide sleep sound mixing, AI-generated bedtime stories, text-to-speech playback, subscription access, language settings, and app support.

3. AI and text-to-speech

SomniaAI may use AI features to generate relaxing bedtime content based on user input. Text-to-speech uses system speech capabilities when available. Users should avoid entering highly sensitive personal information.

4. Data sharing

SomniaAI does not sell sleep habits, journal text, emotion labels, or subscription information to advertising networks. Apple processes purchases under Apple’s own terms and privacy policies.

5. Data retention

Current app preferences and generated content may be stored locally as needed for the app experience. Raw audio should not be stored after speech processing. If cloud AI services are added, data retention will follow the minimum necessary period for providing the feature.

6. Children’s privacy

SomniaAI is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact support.

7. Health disclaimer

SomniaAI is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, clinical diagnosis, or psychological treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, severe insomnia, depression, or self-harm thoughts, please contact licensed professionals or local emergency services immediately.

8. Your choices

You can stop using AI input features, change language preferences, manage subscriptions through your Apple ID, or delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, visit SomniaAI Support. Developer GitHub username: xuezhanying.
