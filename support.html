Subscriptions

Somniary AI Premium is purchased and managed through Apple. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Somniary AI

Restore purchases

Open Somniary AI → Me → Subscription → tap the restore button. Make sure you are signed in with the Apple ID used for purchase.

AI and audio

If audio does not play, check silent mode, volume, Bluetooth output, and Focus settings. AI stories are for relaxation and entertainment only.
